# gt3

High quality DIY 22-input paddle shifted  GT-3 simracing wheel

## Why:
- minimal GT3 format wheel for Logitech G PRO ecosystem, without the squeaking plastic covers and commercial cost cutting.
- reduce awful squeaking noise on high frequency ffb (trueforce) (I can't stress this enough) generated by iRacing
- Easier use for small steering range like Formula Vee
- High quality and stronger paddle shifters.
- Less rotational inertia compared to complete round rims, higher FFB detail
